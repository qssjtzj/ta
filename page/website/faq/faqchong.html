1. What is the "ShowTime coin"?

"ShowTime coin "is a kind of virtual money which can pay online in ShowTime platform. After stored-value for ShowTime coin, users can purchase any service in ShowTime platform anytime, including membership, noble title, virtual gifts and so on.

2. How to use the ShowTime coin?

1. Log in to your ShowTime account;

2. Stored-value for ShowTime coin;

3. Purchase the service you want in ShowTime;

4. Corresponding ShowTime coin value will be deducted in your account;
