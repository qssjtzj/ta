what is "RC coin"?

"RC coin "is a kind of virtual money which can pay online in RC platform. After stored-value for RC coin, users can purchase any service in RC platform anytime, including membership, noble title, virtual gifts and so on.

How to use the RC ?

1. Log in to your RC account;

2. Stored-value for RC coin;

3. Purchase the service you want in RC;

4. Corresponding RC coin value will be deducted in your account;
